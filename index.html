About

Where the code, the console, and the classroom meet.

I'm Divansh, a BCA Core student at Lovely Professional University, running my own freelance web development agency, Dev Globals, out of Punjab, India.

By day I ship client sites and internal tools across PHP, Flutter, and Python. By night I'm building a game world in LÖVE2D, or wiring an ESP32 to something that wasn't originally meant to be smart.

Lately I've been pulled toward the AI side of the stack — retrieval-augmented generation, LLM-backed tooling, and voice agents — and folding it into the automation work I already do with n8n and REST APIs.
